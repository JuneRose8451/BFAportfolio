Portfolio

Florist 2023

A flower shop located in Banica, CA, created using Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop.Through a blend of clean vector lines and textured digital painting, I aimed to capture the warmth and vibrancy of a small-town floral boutique.

Beauty & Pink 2023

This piece explores the connection between femininity, beauty, and empowerment through the color pink. Symbolizing strength, grace, and resilience, the artwork highlights the diverse and radiant essence of women.

City Girls 2023

This piece captures the essence of spontaneity and urban adventure. Inspired by the freedom of wandering without a set plan, it reflects the beauty of embracing the unknown.

Closed Doors 2023

This piece explores the significance of closed doors as a metaphor for growth, faith, and new beginnings. Sometimes, doors remain shut—not as a loss, but as guidance toward something greater.

Make A Deal With Her 2023

Make A Deal With Her - The unpredictability of the game mirrors the complexities of human nature—where deception, skill, and intuition intertwine. The female figure in this work represents confidence and control, embodying the idea that not everything is as it seems

Red Bloom 2025

Red Bloom- The power of transformation through the symbolism of a red bloom. The bold red color represents passion, strength, and the continuous cycle of renewal.

Women Retreat Bookmark 2025

This design is inspired by Hannah, a biblical figure from 1 Samuel 1 & 2, whose story reflects unwavering faith, perseverance, and gratitude to God. She embodies the power of prayer and the joy of trusting in the Lord’s plan. By creating this bookmark for a local church, I aim to share a visual representation of spiritual growth and devotion. The design serves as a reminder of Hannah’s journey—one of hope, answered prayers, and rejoicing in God's faithfulness.

Hannah Bookmark 2025

3D Model :2024

This piece is a surreal fusion of an ice cream cone, a traffic cone, a planet, and a chess piece, created using Blender. Each element symbolizes a contrast between indulgence and strategy—where the sweetness of life meets the challenges we navigate. The ice cream cone represents joy and temptation, while the traffic cone signals obstacles and direction. The planet adds a sense of vastness and exploration, and the chess piece atop it all signifies strategy, decision-making, and intellect. Together, these objects create a whimsical yet thought-provoking composition, reflecting the balance between life’s pleasures and the challenges that shape us.
