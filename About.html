About

My name is Alyssa Guardado, and I grew up in the Bay Area. I am currently studying Digital Media Art (DMA) at San Jose State University, where I immerse myself in the endless possibilities of digital creativity. Art has always been a significant part of my life, not just as a means of expression but as a way of discovering and understanding the world around me. I often find myself completely absorbed in my drawings, losing track of time as I create. This passion drives me to explore new techniques, push creative boundaries, and continuously evolve as an artist.

Beyond my love for art, my faith plays a central role in shaping my perspective and purpose. As a Christian, I find strength and guidance in Proverbs 3:5-6: "Trust in the Lord with all your heart and lean not on your own understanding; in all your ways submit to Him, and He will make your paths straight." This verse serves as a constant reminder that I do not walk this journey alone. My trust in God gives me confidence to embrace challenges, learn from my experiences, and pour my heart into my work.

Art, for me, is an ever-evolving journey of discovery. I am driven by curiosity—the desire to learn, grow, and explore new artistic possibilities. I love experimenting with digital programs, incorporating coding into my creative process, and refining my skills in photography and digital art. The combination of photography and digital manipulation fascinates me because it allows me to blend reality with imagination, creating meaningful visual narratives that tell deeper stories. Each piece in my portfolio reflects a personal experience, emotion, or inspiration that has shaped me. My goal is not just to create visually appealing work but to infuse it with purpose and emotion, making each piece a reflection of something greater.

At the core of my artistic vision is a desire to express love, hope, beauty, and faith. In today’s fast-paced world, it is easy to become overwhelmed by life’s struggles and forget the simple joys that bring meaning to our lives. Through my art, I want to remind people that hope is never out of reach. Whether it is through the symbolism in my compositions or the emotions conveyed in my digital work, I want my creations to uplift, encourage, and inspire. My faith has taught me that even in the darkest moments, there is light, and I want my art to be a reflection of that truth.

After college, I aspire to join an advising or marketing team where I can incorporate my artistic vision to inspire and uplift others. Whether through visual storytelling, branding, or creative campaigns, I want my work to resonate with people on a deeper level, reminding them of the beauty and hope that life has to offer. Ultimately, my mission as an artist is to create work that not only reflects my personal journey but also speaks to and uplifts those who encounter it.
